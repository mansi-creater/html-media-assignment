Google Fonts

Google Fonts are a collection of high-quality web fonts for use on web projects. Additionally, all fonts can be downloaded for installation on local systems. Fonts are pulled into your website through Google’s content delivery network (CDN) and will load automatically once installed.
Google Fonts makes it easy to bring personality and performance to your websites and products. Our robust catalog of open-source fonts and icons, makes it easy to integrate expressive type and icons seamlessly—no matter where you are in the world.

Google Fonts (formerly known as Google Web Fonts) is a computer font and web font service owned by Google. This includes free and open source font families, an interactive web directory for browsing the library. Google Fonts is also used With the Google Workspace (Such as Docs, Sheets, Drawings and Slides).
